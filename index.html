How To Play

What is UNBEATEN: 50-0?

A boxing career roguelite. You build a fighter from the attributes of boxing legends, add traits, then try to run an unbeaten 50-0 career against escalating opposition.

Building a fighter / card

From the menu choose Draft New Boxer. You spin to land on a source legend, then lock one of their attributes onto your fighter. Repeat until all ten attributes are filled, then roll your traits.

How attributes work

Every attribute is rated 0-99 and colour-coded by tier: gold 95+ (premium), orange 90-94 (legendary), purple 85-89 (luxury), blue 80-84 (rare), white 75-79 (normal), tan below 75. Higher is better, and matching certain attribute sources unlocks hidden synergies.

How traits work

Traits are perks (or flaws) rolled during the draft. Their colour shows rarity — grey common, green uncommon, blue rare, purple epic, orange legendary, gold premium — while red marks negative or cursed traits that come with a downside.

What is Play Money?

Play Money is the in-game currency you earn from fights and milestones. It is used for cosmetic card items in the Store. It is not real money and is never purchased.

Card cosmetics & the Store

The Store and Card Editor let you customise your fighter card with cosmetic frames, auras, backgrounds and characters. These are visual only and do not change fight outcomes.

Leaderboards & cloud saves

Signing in lets your careers sync to the cloud, appear on global leaderboards, and be viewed by other players. Your progress is saved locally as well.

UNBEATEN: 50-0 is in active development. Some features are early access and may change as the game improves.
